## Agent CTA Writing Rules

1. **Match funnel stage to lead magnet** — don't pitch a strategy call in an awareness post
2. **One CTA per piece** — never stack two offers in one caption
3. **Use the keyword system** — "DM me [keyword]" keeps tracking clean for attribution
4. **Platform tone:**
   - Reels caption → short, punchy, 1 CTA line
   - Thread / long-form → CTA at end after value delivery
   - Story → direct, swipe-up or DM
5. **Vertical matching** — always confirm the content vertical before picking the lead magnet
