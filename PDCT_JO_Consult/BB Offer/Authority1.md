Here’s how to recreate this specific "Authority" format:

---

## 1. The Direct Hook (0:00 – 0:08)

- **Visual:** Centered, eye-level shot with a high-quality microphone prominent in the frame.
    
- **Text:** Words appear one by one in the center of the screen as he speaks, highlighting key terms like **"goal,"** **"go-to-name,"** **"seller,"** and **"brand."**
    
- **The "Shift":** The hook identifies a common desire (being the top name in a niche) and immediately challenges the viewer's mindset (stop being a seller, start being a brand).
    

## 2. The "3-Pillar" Framework (0:09 – 0:37)

Instead of a long monologue, the video breaks down the advice into three numbered points. This keeps the viewer engaged because they know exactly how much value they are getting.

- **Point 1: Invest in Audience (0:09 – 0:16)**
    
    - **Visual B-Roll:** A quick cut to a cinematic shot of the creator working in a dark room with headphones. This creates a "behind-the-scenes" feel.
        
- **Point 2: Build Proof (0:17 – 0:27)**
    
    - **Visual Social Proof:** Rapid-fire screenshots of Instagram profiles, follower counts, and testimonials. This is "proof" in action—it shows that the strategies actually work.
        
- **Point 3: Premium Presence (0:28 – 0:37)**
    
    - **Lifestyle B-Roll:** Dynamic shots of the creator in a gym, editing a video, or looking out a window. This sells the _result_ of the success, not just the process.
        

## 3. The Technical Look

- **Lighting:** Professional "Rembrandt" lighting (one side of the face is lit, the other is in soft shadow) to create depth and a cinematic feel.
    
- **Audio:** Crisp, studio-quality sound with a slight "radio" bass effect.
    
- **Text Overlays:** Minimalist, bold white text that is perfectly synced with the speech.

## How to Adapt This for Your Agent

### Step 2: The Proof Section

- Instead of talking about the product, **show** it.
    
- **Visual:** Flash screenshots of happy client emails, closing statements, or the agent on the phone winning a deal. This provides immediate credibility.
    

### Step 3: The CTA (The Final Word)

- End with a powerful "closing statement" like the video does: _"Ads get you attention, brands get you paid."_
    
- **Agent CTA:** "Follow me for more strategies on [Niche] or click the link in my bio to work with me."
