# JO Consult: Consultation Delivery SOP 📋

To deliver professional consultations while working a 9-5 at Savasa, you need a high-leverage delivery system.

## 1. Pre-Consultation (Automation)
- **Qualifier:** Ensure the client has filled out the `Onboarding_Questionnaire.md` (to be created).
- **Audit:** Use `AI_Tools/content_analyze_viral_reel.py` to audit their current content before the call.

## 2. During the Consultation (The Framework)
1. **Diagnosis (10 min):** Review their "Consistency Roadblocks".
2. **Strategy (20 min):** Map out their "High-Performance Content" using the Ruben/Fadli style.
3. **Handover (10 min):** Give them their specific "Script Template" and "Asset Index".

## 3. Post-Consultation (Documentation)
- Save all call notes in `PDCT_JO_Consult/zz Client Delivery/[Client_Name]/`.
- Share the **Asset Index** so they know what b-roll to use.
