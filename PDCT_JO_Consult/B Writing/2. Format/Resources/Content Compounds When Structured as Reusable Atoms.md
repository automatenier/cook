# Content Compounds When Structured as Reusable Atoms

## The Idea
One piece of long-form content, broken into atomic units (a hook, a claim, a story, a CTA), can be recombined into 5x the output without 5x the effort. The same is true for notes — atomic ideas recombine into new thoughts.

## The parallel
- Content atom → repurposed into 5 posts
- Evergreen note → referenced in 10 future notes
Both grow in value the more they connect to other things.

## Applied to my content system
- One client interview → hook bank, story bank, CTA bank → 30+ pieces
- Tool: `tools/repurpose_content.py` automates the recombination

## Applied to this vault
- Notes titled as complete statements → reusable as building blocks
- Linked notes → new ideas emerge from connections, not just storage
