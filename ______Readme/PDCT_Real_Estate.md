# 🏡 PDCT_Real_Estate: Personal Brand Hub
This folder is where you brainstorm, script, and storyboard content for your property brand (Savasa, etc.).

## 🚀 Key Commands for this Folder

| Command | Action | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **`/render-canvas`** | Triggers `content_render_full_canvas.py` | Turn your visual storyboard into a video. |
| **`/sync-kanban`** | Triggers `content_kanban_sync.py` | Push your "Scheduled" cards to your Excel tracker. |
| **`/roi-sim`** | Triggers `cook_roi_simulation.py` | Generate investment simulations for a property. |

## 📁 Folder Structure Breakdown:
- **`__CMD/`**: Your Control Center. Contains `Content Kanban.md` to track all reels.
- **`C Storyboarding/`**: Your "Studio." Contains `.canvas` files where you layout your videos.
- **`B Writing/`**: Where your scripts and hooks are drafted.
- **`E. Setter/`**: Where your real estate sales scripts and DMs are kept.

## 💡 How to use:
When you are in the **PDCT_Real_Estate** folder, tell Gemini:
> "Gemini, check my Real Estate Kanban and sync it." or "Gemini, render the Savasa Canvas."
