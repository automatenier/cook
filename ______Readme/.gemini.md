# 🤖 .gemini: The AI Brain Configuration
This hidden folder controls how I (Gemini) behave, what custom commands I know, and what special skills I can use.

## 📁 Folder Breakdown

### 1. `commands/`
These are the "shortcuts" you use with a forward slash `/`.
- **Core Commands (Root):**
    - `/download-reel`: Downloads videos from URLs.
    - `/posts`: Uploads to all platforms.
    - `/render-reel`: Connects to Remotion/Modal for rendering.
    - `/next`: Shows your daily business agenda.
    - `/create-captions`: Generates transparent overlays for video.
- **`commands/test/` Folder:**
    - A massive library of experimental workflows (40+ files) for script writing, YouTube repurposing, and discovery call prep. 
    - *Note: These are advanced "test" workflows for specific business scenarios.*

### 2. `skills/`
These are specialized "Power-Ups" that I can activate to perform complex tasks.
- **`source-analyst`**: Analyzes PPTX, PDF, and MD files to give you deep insights (inspired by NotebookLM).
- **`pptx-generator`**: Allows me to create professional PowerPoint slides directly from your notes.
- **`excalidraw-gen`**: Helps in generating visual diagrams or sketches.

### 3. `policies/`
- **`safety.toml`**: The rules that ensure I operate safely and protect your credentials.

### 4. `settings.json`
- The core configuration file that tells me which tools are available and how to connect to them.

---

## 🚀 How to use Gemini "Commands"
You don't need to navigate into this folder. Simply type the command in the chat:

> **Example:** `/download-reel https://tiktok.com/video123`
> **Example:** `/next` (to see your morning brief)
> **Example:** `/posts file="final.mp4" title="New Savasa Home"`
