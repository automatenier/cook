# Cold Outreach — Fitness Trainers

> Email/DM templates for outreaching fitness trainers (JO Consult prospects). Based on the 25% Reply Formula.

---

## The 25% Reply Formula

**Personalization + Value Prop + Niche + Their Goal + Their Value Prop + Case Study + Cliffhanger + CTA**

Every outreach message must hit these 8 elements in order. Miss one and reply rate drops.

---

## Personalization Cheat Sheet

How to fill in the variables quickly:

| Variable | Where to Find | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `{{specific_content_compliment}}` | Check their last 3 reels | "Reel tentang progressive overload itu on point" |
| `{{engagement_metric}}` | Look at likes/comments/views | "2K views", "150 likes" |
| `{{platform}}` | Where you found them | "IG", "TikTok", "YouTube" |
| `{{observation_about_hustle}}` | Bio / stories / posts | "lo handle clients 1-on-1 dari pagi sampe malem" |
| `{{case_study_before}}` | From real client results | "5 Juta/bulan" |
| `{{case_study_after}}` | From real client results | "52 Juta/bulan" |
| `{{current_revenue}}` | Estimate from their activity | "10-15 Juta" |

## Channel Adaptation

| Channel | Max Length | Tone Adjustment |
|---|---|---|
| **Email** | Full template above | Formal-casual, include subject line |
| **Instagram DM** | 3-4 sentences max | Drop subject line, more casual, reference their specific post |
| **WhatsApp** | 2-3 sentences | Super casual, voice note option for follow-up |
| **LinkedIn** | 3-4 sentences | Slightly more professional, mention "online coaching business" |

## Rules
- Always lead with genuine personalization (never generic "I love your content")
- One CTA only — never double-CTA
- Max 3 follow-ups then stop
- Never pitch price in outreach — only in call
- Track everything in CRM (Google Sheets / Excel)
