# Cold Scraping SOP — Lead Discovery Methods

> Source: Main Drive vault. Supplements the organic setter DM outreach workflow with cold scraping techniques.

---

## 3 Scraping Methods

### Method 1: Keyword Search
- Tentukan keyword/hashtag relevan ke ICP
- Visual check 3 detik pada setiap profil: foto profil + bio
- **Example ICP (Fitness):** 40+, overweight, has a job → QUALIFIED
- Physical cues: belly fat, "dad bod", tired expression
- Financial cues: family vacations, golf, nice cars, corporate attire

### Method 2: Hijack Comments (Bajak Komentar)
- Cari komentar di postingan competitor/niche accounts
- Visual check 3 detik pada profil komentator
- Orang yang komentar = actively engaged di topik = higher intent
- Same ICP criteria as Method 1

### Method 3: Hijack Followers (Bajak Followers)
- Orang yang follow competitor = interested di topik serupa
- Mereka akan interested kalau kamu offer value sama atau lebih baik
- Most scalable method — large pool of leads

## Approach Flow

**Key Rule:** "Berinteraksi seperti Manusia" — JANGAN ada bawaan mau jual

| Fase | Action | Timing |
|------|--------|--------|
| View Profile | Check content mereka | Day 0 |
| Follow + Likes | Warm up 3-5 posts | Day 0 |
| Like Story | Engage with stories | Day 1 |
| Balas Story | Start conversation naturally | Day 1-2 |

After Phase 4 → transition to Setter DM Outreach Workflow Stage 3 (Conversation)

## Warm Outreach Tactics (Creative)

- **AI avatar outreach** — personalized video messages
- **WhatsApp quota** — bulk warm outreach via WA
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — B2B fitness industry leads
- **Live TikTok campaign** — engage live viewers → DM follow-up
- **IG follow → warm → warm approach** — multi-touch before DM
- **AI Songs** — "Quick question, what's your favorite music? Can I sing to you with my AI Clone?"

## Cold Email Template

**Subject:** Quick chat about [Prospect's Company]?

> Hi [Name],
> I noticed your company is [relevant detail about their industry/growth/news].
> We help companies like yours achieve [specific result or benefit].
> I'd love to schedule a quick call to explore how [product/service] could support your goals.
> Let me know if next Tuesday or Wednesday works.

**Strategy:** Create engagement first → after they lower guards → irresistible offer
