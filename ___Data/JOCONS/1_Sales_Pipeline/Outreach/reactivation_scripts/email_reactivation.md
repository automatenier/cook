# Email Reactivation Sequences

> 3-email sequence per temperature. Sent over 7 days. Customize placeholders: `{NAME}`, `{PAIN_POINT}`, `{OFFER}`, `{RESULT}`, `{CLIENT_NAME}`, `{FREE_RESOURCE}`, `{YOUR_NAME}`, `{CTA_LINK}`.

---

## HOT Leads (< 30 days) — 3-Email Sequence

### Email 1 (Day 1) — "Ada yang baru"
**Subject:** {NAME}, ada update yang mungkin relevan buat lo
**Preview:** Sejak terakhir kita ngobrol, gue dapet result baru...

```
Hey {NAME},

Gue inget kita pernah ngobrol soal {PAIN_POINT}.

Sejak itu, banyak yang berubah. Salah satunya: {CLIENT_NAME} baru aja {RESULT} pakai system yang gue develop.

Gue bikin free {FREE_RESOURCE_TYPE} yang breakdown exactly gimana prosesnya: {CTA_LINK}

Kalau lo masih di situasi yang sama, ini bisa jadi game-changer.

Talk soon,
{YOUR_NAME}
```

### Email 2 (Day 3) — Social proof
**Subject:** Gimana {CLIENT_NAME} {RESULT} dalam 30 hari
**Preview:** Dan kenapa ini bisa applicable buat lo juga...

```
{NAME},

Quick story:

{CLIENT_NAME} datang ke gue dengan masalah yang sama persis kayak lo — {PAIN_POINT}.

30 hari kemudian, dia {RESULT}.

Yang bikin beda bukan "kerja lebih keras." Yang bikin beda: {UNIQUE_MECHANISM_ONE_LINER}.

Mau tau lebih detail? Reply email ini atau book 15-min chat: {CALENDAR_LINK}

No pitch, just clarity.

{YOUR_NAME}
```

### Email 3 (Day 7) — Last touch
**Subject:** Last thing — then I'll stop bothering you 😄
**Preview:** Free resource + no strings attached

```
{NAME},

Ini email terakhir gue soal ini.

Gue gak mau jadi "that guy" yang spam inbox. Tapi gue genuinely think gue bisa bantu.

Here's what I'll leave you with:
→ Free {FREE_RESOURCE}: {CTA_LINK}
→ 15-min call kalau mau discuss: {CALENDAR_LINK}

No pressure, no expiry. Kapan aja lo ready, gue di sini.

Semoga coaching-nya makin sukses 💪

{YOUR_NAME}
```

## WARM Leads (30-90 days) — 3-Email Sequence

### Email 1 (Day 1) — Reconnect
**Subject:** {NAME} — masih coaching?
**Preview:** Udah lama gak connect, gue mau share something...

```
Hey {NAME},

Udah beberapa bulan sejak terakhir kita connect.

Gue cuma mau check in — masih coaching? How's it going?

Di sisi gue, banyak yang berkembang. Gue udah bantu {NUMBER} coaches build full content + sales system, dan hasilnya consistently {AVERAGE_RESULT}.

Gue juga baru bikin free {FREE_RESOURCE_TYPE}: "{FREE_RESOURCE_TITLE}"

Specifically buat coaches yang {ICP_DESCRIPTION}.

Download di sini (free, no sign up): {CTA_LINK}

Semoga bermanfaat!

{YOUR_NAME}
```

### Email 2 (Day 4) — One tip
**Subject:** 1 tip yang bikin {CLIENT_NAME} {MICRO_RESULT}
**Preview:** Takes 10 minutes, zero cost...

```
{NAME},

Satu tip cepat yang gue share ke semua coaches gue:

{ONE_ACTIONABLE_TIP — 2-3 paragraphs max}

{CLIENT_NAME} implement ini dan langsung {MICRO_RESULT} dalam seminggu.

Kalau lo mau full system-nya (bukan cuma 1 tip), gue bisa breakdown di call 15 menit: {CALENDAR_LINK}

Atau reply email ini — gue baca semua replies personally.

{YOUR_NAME}
```

### Email 3 (Day 7) — Soft close
**Subject:** Open invitation (no expiry)
**Preview:** Whenever you're ready...

```
{NAME},

Gue gak mau push — tapi gue mau make sure lo tau:

Kalau lo ever butuh bantuan dengan {PAIN_POINT}, gue di sini.

What I can help with:
• {DELIVERABLE_1}
• {DELIVERABLE_2}
• {DELIVERABLE_3}

Book call kapan aja: {CALENDAR_LINK}
Free resource: {CTA_LINK}

No rush. Wishing you the best 🙏

{YOUR_NAME}
```

## COLD Leads (90+ days) — 3-Email Sequence

### Email 1 (Day 1) — Pure value, zero pitch
**Subject:** Free guide: "{FREE_RESOURCE_TITLE}"
**Preview:** No pitch, no upsell. Just something useful.

```
Hey {NAME},

Gue {YOUR_NAME}. Kita pernah connect beberapa waktu lalu.

Gue baru bikin free guide tentang "{FREE_RESOURCE_TITLE}" — {ONE_LINE_DESCRIPTION}.

Download link: {CTA_LINK}

Gak ada upsell, gak ada email sequence setelah ini. Murni value.

Semoga useful!

{YOUR_NAME}
```

### Email 2 (Day 5) — Story-based
**Subject:** Dari 0 klien ke {RESULT} — kisah {CLIENT_NAME}
**Preview:** Coach yang hampir nyerah, tapi...

```
{NAME},

Quick story yang gue mau share:

{CLIENT_NAME} hampir quit coaching {TIME} lalu. {BRIEF_STRUGGLE_STORY — 2-3 sentences}.

Sekarang dia {CURRENT_RESULT}.

Yang berubah bukan skill-nya — yang berubah sistem-nya.

Kalau lo penasaran gimana, reply aja email ini. Gue ceritain.

{YOUR_NAME}
```

### Email 3 (Day 10) — Final, graceful
**Subject:** (final email) — pintu tetap terbuka
**Preview:** No more emails after this, promise.

```
{NAME},

Ini email terakhir dari gue. Promise 😄

Summary singkat:
- Free resource kalau butuh: {CTA_LINK}
- 15-min call kalau mau ngobrol: {CALENDAR_LINK}
- Reply email ini kapan aja — gue always read.

Kalau coaching bukan fokus lo lagi, totally respect that.
Kalau masih, gue di sini whenever you're ready.

All the best,
{YOUR_NAME}

P.S. Unsubscribe kapan aja. No hard feelings.
```

---

## Email Tips

- **Subject lines:** Keep under 50 chars. Use their name. Avoid spam words (FREE, GUARANTEED, ACT NOW)
- **Preview text:** This is the second hook — make it count
- **Length:** Max 150 words per email for cold. Slightly longer OK for hot.
- **CTA:** ONE clear CTA per email. Don't give 5 options.
- **Sending:** Use personal email (not marketing tool) for hot leads. Looks more genuine.
- **Reply-to:** Always your real email. Personal replies build trust.
