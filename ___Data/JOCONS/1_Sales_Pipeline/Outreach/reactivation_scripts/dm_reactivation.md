# DM Reactivation Scripts — Instagram

> 3 variants per temperature. Customize placeholders: `{NAME}`, `{PAIN_POINT}`, `{OFFER}`, `{RESULT}`, `{CLIENT_NAME}`, `{FREE_RESOURCE}`.

---

## HOT Leads (< 30 days since last contact)

### Variant 1: "Ada update"
```
Hey {NAME}! Gue inget kita pernah ngobrol soal {PAIN_POINT} beberapa minggu lalu.

Sejak itu gue udah dapet result baru yang gila — {CLIENT_NAME} baru aja {RESULT} dalam 30 hari pertama.

Gue bikin sesuatu yang mungkin bantu lo juga. Mau gue share?
```

### Variant 2: Story reply reconnect
```
{NAME}! Story lo tadi resonated banget sama gue.

Btw gue baru aja launch something baru yang specifically address {PAIN_POINT} — exactly yang lo ceritain waktu itu.

Kalau masih relevan, mau ngobrol sebentar?
```

### Variant 3: Value-first
```
Yo {NAME}, gue baru bikin {FREE_RESOURCE} yang breakdown gimana cara {SPECIFIC_OUTCOME}.

Inget lo pernah mention soal {PAIN_POINT} — ini literally addresses that.

Mau gue kirim? Free, no strings.
```

---

## WARM Leads (30-90 days)

### Variant 1: "Gue notice"
```
Hey {NAME}! Udah lama gak ngobrol.

Gue liat content lo akhir-akhir ini — {SPECIFIC_OBSERVATION_ABOUT_THEIR_CONTENT}.

Gue udah banyak belajar sejak terakhir kita chat. Ada method baru yang bisa help coaches kayak lo {SPECIFIC_BENEFIT}. Curious gak?
```

### Variant 2: Social proof drop
```
{NAME}! Random thought — gue baru liat hasil {CLIENT_NAME} yang incredible banget.

Dia mulai dari situasi yang mirip sama lo waktu itu ({PAIN_POINT}), dan sekarang udah {RESULT}.

Gue document prosesnya. Mau gue share? Mungkin bisa applicable buat lo juga.
```

### Variant 3: Free resource offer
```
Hey {NAME}, long time!

Gue baru bikin free {FREE_RESOURCE_TYPE} tentang {TOPIC}. Specifically buat coaches yang {ICP_DESCRIPTION}.

Mau gue kirim linknya? Gak perlu sign up atau apa-apa.
```

### Follow-up (Day 3, if no reply):
```
{NAME} no pressure ya — just thought of you pas bikin ini.

Kalau timing-nya belum pas, totally get it. Resource-nya tetep ada kalau nanti butuh 🙏
```

## COLD Leads (90+ days)

### Variant 1: "Soft re-intro"
```
Hey {NAME}! Ini {YOUR_NAME}. Kita pernah connect beberapa bulan lalu.

Banyak yang berubah di sisi gue — gue udah build system baru yang bantu coaches {SPECIFIC_BENEFIT} tanpa harus {COMMON_FRUSTRATION}.

Just checking in — masih coaching? How's it going?
```

### Variant 2: "Thought of you"
```
{NAME}! Random — gue lagi bantu coach yang situasinya mirip sama lo dulu, dan gue jadi inget conversation kita.

Lo masih {COACHING_ACTIVITY}? Genuinely curious gimana perkembangannya.
```

### Variant 3: Pure value (no pitch)
```
Hey {NAME}, gue bikin free guide tentang "{FREE_RESOURCE_TITLE}".

Gak ada pitch, gak ada upsell. Pure value.

Kalau lo masih di coaching space, might be useful. Mau gue send?
```

### Follow-up (Day 7, if no reply):
```
{NAME} — no worries kalau busy. Just sharing satu insight cepat:

{ONE_LINE_TIP_RELEVANT_TO_THEIR_NICHE}

Kalau mau full breakdown, ada di guide yang gue mention. Take care! 💪
```

---

## Response Handling

### If they reply positively:
→ Transition to normal DM flow (setter takes over at Stage 3+)

### If they reply "not now":
```
Totally get it {NAME}. Gue save chat ini — kalau nanti situasinya berubah, tinggal reach out aja. No expiry date.

Semoga coaching-nya makin lancar! 💪
```

### If they reply with objection/skepticism:
```
Fair question. Gue gak mau push anything — yang gue bisa share adalah {SOCIAL_PROOF_ONE_LINER}.

Tapi kalau lo curious, gue bisa jelaskan lebih detail di call 15 menit. No commitment. Mau?
```

### If they ask price immediately:
```
Good question! Tergantung paket — tapi sebelum harga, gue mau pastiin ini cocok buat situasi lo dulu.

Bisa ceritain sebentar: sekarang coaching lo kayak gimana? Berapa klien, dan target lo apa?
```
