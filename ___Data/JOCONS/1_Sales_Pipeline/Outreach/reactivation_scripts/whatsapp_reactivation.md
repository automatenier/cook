# WhatsApp Reactivation Scripts

> WhatsApp = more personal, higher open rate. Keep messages shorter and more casual than DM. Customize placeholders: `{NAME}`, `{PAIN_POINT}`, `{OFFER}`, `{RESULT}`, `{CLIENT_NAME}`, `{FREE_RESOURCE}`.

---

## HOT Leads (< 30 days)

### Variant 1: Voice note style (text version)
```
{NAME}! Bro/sis, gue baru dapet result yang gila — {CLIENT_NAME} {RESULT} dalam sebulan.

Inget lo pernah tanya soal {PAIN_POINT}? Gue udah develop system baru yang specifically handle itu.

Mau gue jelasin sebentar? 5 menit aja.
```

### Variant 2: Direct value
```
Hey {NAME}! Gue bikin {FREE_RESOURCE} khusus buat coach yang struggle sama {PAIN_POINT}.

Mau gue kirim? Free kok.
```

### Variant 3: Referral angle
```
{NAME}! Lo tau gak, minggu lalu gue bantu coach yang situasinya PERSIS kayak lo ceritain waktu itu.

Hasilnya {RESULT}. Gue langsung kepikiran lo.

Masih butuh bantuan di area itu gak?
```

## WARM Leads (30-90 days)

### Variant 1: Check-in casual
```
Hey {NAME}, apa kabar? Udah lama gak chat.

Gimana coaching-nya? Masih {THEIR_ACTIVITY}?

Gue ada update — banyak yang berubah di sisi gue. Kalau mau catch up, let me know 🙏
```

### Variant 2: Value bomb
```
{NAME}! Gue baru publish sesuatu yang mungkin useful buat lo:

"{FREE_RESOURCE_TITLE}"

Link: {LINK}

Semoga bantu! Kalau ada pertanyaan, chat aja langsung di sini.
```

### Variant 3: Testimonial share
```
{NAME}! Gue mau share something.

{CLIENT_NAME} — coach yang mirip sama lo — baru aja {RESULT}.

Dia mulai dari {SIMILAR_STARTING_POINT}. Sistemnya sama yang gue develop baru-baru ini.

Kalau curious gimana prosesnya, gue bisa ceritain.
```

## COLD Leads (90+ days)

### Variant 1: Soft reconnect
```
Hey {NAME}! Ini {YOUR_NAME}. Kita pernah chat beberapa bulan lalu.

Just checking — masih di coaching space? Genuinely curious gimana perkembangannya.

No pitch, just wanna reconnect 🙏
```

### Variant 2: Milestone share
```
{NAME}! Random update — gue baru aja hit milestone: {YOUR_MILESTONE}.

Banyak yang berubah sejak terakhir kita ngobrol. Kalau lo masih coaching, gue punya beberapa insights baru yang might help.

How's everything going di sisi lo?
```

### Variant 3: Free resource (zero pitch)
```
Hey {NAME}, long time!

Gue bikin free guide: "{FREE_RESOURCE_TITLE}"

{ONE_LINE_DESCRIPTION}.

Link: {LINK}

Gak perlu sign up. Semoga bermanfaat!
```

---

## WhatsApp-Specific Tips

- **Timing:** Send 08:00-10:00 or 19:00-21:00 (WIB)
- **Voice notes:** Even better than text. Record a 30-second personalized voice note instead of text when possible
- **No broadcast lists:** Send individual messages. Broadcast feels impersonal.
- **Read receipts:** If they read but don't reply, wait 3 days before follow-up
- **Status updates:** Post value content to WA Status — reactivation leads will see it
- **Profile photo:** Make sure your WA profile looks professional and matches your IG
