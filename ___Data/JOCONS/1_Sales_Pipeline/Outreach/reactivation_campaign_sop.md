# Reactivation Campaign SOP — "Reviving Past Leads"

> Protocol untuk client yang punya database lama (old DMs, email list, WA contacts) yang belum pernah beli. Diberikan ke client di Day 10-12 sebagai bagian dari onboarding.

---

## Overview

Setiap coach punya leads yang pernah tertarik tapi belum closing. Mereka mungkin:
- Pernah DM tanya harga tapi ghosting
- Subscribe email tapi gak pernah book call
- Engage di content tapi gak pernah reach out
- Pernah ikut free webinar / download lead magnet

**Goal:** Re-engage leads ini dengan offer baru yang lebih kuat + social proof yang fresh.

---

## Step 1: Export & Segment Leads

### Cara export:
- **Instagram DMs:** Scroll melalui inbox, catat nama + last interaction date
- **Email list:** Export dari email tool (Mailchimp, etc) ke spreadsheet
- **WhatsApp:** Scroll contacts yang pernah chat tentang coaching
- **Webinar attendees:** Export dari platform webinar

### Segmentasi (3 temperature):

| Segment | Kriteria | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **HOT** | Interaksi terakhir < 30 hari. Pernah tanya harga atau book call tapi gak jadi. | Highest — reach out HARI INI |
| **WARM** | Interaksi 30-90 hari lalu. Pernah engage, download lead magnet, atau reply stories. | Medium — reach out minggu ini |
| **COLD** | 90+ hari tidak kontak. Pernah follow atau engage tapi sudah lama. | Lowest — reach out dalam 2 minggu |

### Template Spreadsheet:

| Nama | Platform | Last Contact | Segment | Pain Point (jika tahu) | Status | Response |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Coach Andi | IG DM | 2 minggu lalu | HOT | Gak bisa dapet leads | Sent | Replied ✅ |
| Coach Budi | WhatsApp | 2 bulan lalu | WARM | Content inconsistent | Sent | No reply |
| Coach Citra | Email | 4 bulan lalu | COLD | Unknown | Pending | — |

---

## Step 2: Craft Messages Per Segment

> Prinsip utama: JANGAN langsung jualan. Lead this with value + genuine concern. Tunjukkan bahwa sesuatu sudah berubah sejak terakhir mereka ngobrol.

### Timing Rules:
- Send max 10-15 messages per hari (jangan spam)
- Best time: 08:00-10:00 atau 19:00-21:00
- Jangan kirim di weekend (kecuali WA casual)
- Space out per platform — jangan blast semua sekaligus

### Follow-Up Cadence:
- Day 1: Initial message
- Day 3: Follow up if no reply (different angle)
- Day 7: Value bomb (share tip / content yang relevan)
- Day 14: Final attempt or archive
- **3 strikes rule:** Jika tidak reply setelah 3 follow-ups → archive. Jangan chase.

---

## Step 3: Execute Per Channel

### Channel Priority Order:
1. **Instagram DM** — highest engagement, most personal
2. **WhatsApp** — direct, high open rate
3. **Email** — scalable, lowest response rate

### Rules:
- Personalize EVERY message — reference something specific about them
- Use their name
- Reference last interaction if possible
- Lead with what's NEW (new offer, new results, new social proof)
- CTA = low commitment (free resource, quick chat, watch a video)

---

## Step 4: Track & Optimize

### After each batch (10-15 messages):
- [ ] Update spreadsheet: Sent / Replied / Booked / No Reply
- [ ] Calculate reply rate per segment
- [ ] Identify which script variant works best
- [ ] Adjust messaging for next batch

### Expected benchmarks:

| Metric | HOT | WARM | COLD |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reply rate | 30-50% | 15-25% | 5-10% |
| Book rate (from replies) | 40-60% | 20-30% | 10-20% |
| Close rate (from bookings) | 30-50% | 20-30% | 15-25% |

---

## Step 5: Handoff to Setter

Once a reactivated lead replies positively:
1. Setter takes over the conversation
2. Follow normal DM flow (Stage 3+ from setter workflow)
3. Label in Instagram: based on stage
4. Track in CRM with source: "Reactivation - [HOT/WARM/COLD]"

---

## When to Run Reactivation Campaigns

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| New client onboarded (Day 10-12) | Run initial reactivation on their old database |
| Every 2 weeks (ongoing) | Refresh reactivation with new social proof |
| After major milestone (e.g., client gets big result) | Blast new testimonial to warm + cold leads |
| After new lead magnet launch | Share with all segments |
| Seasonal (Ramadan, New Year, etc.) | Special angle reactivation |
