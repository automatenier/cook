# Belief Chain Framework — Content Strategy by Funnel Stage

> Maps the beliefs your ICP must adopt — from stranger to buyer. Each belief = a content category. Content moves them through the chain.

---

## How It Works

Your ICP doesn't go from "never heard of you" to "take my money" in one step. They move through a **chain of beliefs**. Each belief unlocks the next stage. Your content's job is to shift ONE belief at a time.

```
Stranger → TOF → MOF → BOF → Buyer
         (aware) (trust) (ready) (pays)
```

---

## 1) Core Problem-Aware Belief (TOF)

**The belief they must adopt:**
> "If I stay [doing what I'm doing], I'll always be capped — no matter how good I am."

**Who this targets:**
- Inconsistent beginners OR already making money but feel capped / burnt out / limited by time + structure
- They know (or fear) there's no real exit plan... they just don't know how to get out

**Source sheets:** Dream Follower/ICP Sheet, Client's Story

**TOF content styles:**
- "Behind the [current identity] forever" reality checks (time cap, body cap, life cap)
- "Transition season" talk: talented, hungry, but stuck in inconsistent execution
- Pattern interrupts that call out their exact situation

**Content goal:** Make them FEEL the problem. Not teach — trigger.

---

## 2) Bridge Belief #1 — Solution Aware (TOF → MOF)

**The belief they must adopt:**
> "My real problem isn't motivation — it's lack of structure + clarity, so I consume but don't execute."

**Who this targets:**
- People who resonate with Belief #1 and now want to understand WHY they're stuck

**ICP literally calls out:** lack structure/clarity/consistency, consuming content but not executing, wanting guidance without being delayed

**Source sheets:** Dream Follower/ICP Sheet

**TOF/MOF content styles:**
- "You're not lazy, you're unstructured" reframes (in client's tone)
- Before/after routines: what changes when you have a clear direction + structure
- "I used to think I needed more motivation, then I realized I needed a system"

**Content goal:** Shift blame from THEM to their SITUATION. Make your method feel like the missing piece.

---

## 3) Bridge Belief #2 — Solution Aware (MOF)

**The belief they must adopt:**
> "It's not 'post more'... it's 'be seen' — people buy the person behind the brand."

**Who this targets:**
- People who already know they need help but haven't committed to anyone yet

**Key insight:** Client's turning point was when they stopped hiding, showed themselves + the process, and views/results shifted.

**The frame:** [Your craft] is trust — they want to know WHO they're choosing. People buy the person, not the product.

**Source sheets:** Client's Story

**MOF content styles:**
- Face + process content ("watch me work" proof)
- "How I positioned myself differently" breakdowns (what changed when they stopped blending in)
- Behind-the-scenes that show competence + personality

**Content goal:** Build TRUST. Make them feel "I want to work with THIS person specifically."

---

## 4) Bridge Belief #3 — Opportunity Aware (MOF → BOF)

**The belief they must adopt:**
> "High-level clients (and high-level pricing) come from credibility + professionalism + discretion — not just aesthetics."

**Key insight:** The industry side is relationship-driven & credibility-based; reputation travels fast.

**Source sheets:** Industry Identity Sheet

**MOF/BOF content styles:**
- "Play your cards carefully" content (approval, discretion, not twisting content)
- Professionalism > flashiness positioning
- Case studies showing how credibility = premium pricing

**Content goal:** Position your offer as the PREMIUM, credible choice. Eliminate cheaper alternatives.

---

## 5) Decision Belief — Ready to Buy (BOF)

**The belief they must adopt:**
> "This person/program is the one that can actually get me there — and the cost of waiting is higher than the cost of starting."

**BOF content styles:**
- Testimonials / transformations with SPECIFIC results
- "Here's what happened in 30/60/90 days" timelines
- FAQ-style objection busters (price, time, "is this for me?")
- Soft CTA stories: "DM me [keyword]" or "link in bio"
- Urgency (limited spots, cohort closing, price increase)

**Content goal:** Remove final hesitation. Make buying feel obvious and safe.

## How to Build a Belief Chain for ANY Client

### Step 1: Fill out Brand Sheets (all 7)
### Step 2: Answer these per belief:

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | What does your ICP currently believe that keeps them stuck? | |
| 2 | What's the FIRST thing they need to realize? | |
| 3 | What's the SECOND shift (from "I need help" to "I need THIS kind of help")? | |
| 4 | What's the THIRD shift (from "this seems good" to "this is the ONE")? | |
| 5 | What makes them finally pull the trigger? | |

### Step 3: Write 5-10 content ideas per belief stage
### Step 4: Map to content calendar (rotate through chain weekly)
