# Content Audit Checklist — Scoring Framework

> Score each of the client's last 20 posts across 5 dimensions. Total score out of 50 per post.

---

## How to Use

1. Pull client's last 20 Instagram posts (caption + metrics)
2. Score each post using the framework below
3. Calculate averages per dimension
4. Identify top 3 posts and bottom 3 posts
5. Output findings into audit report

---

## Scoring Dimensions (1-10 each)

### 1. Hook Quality (1-10)
| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | No hook / generic opener / starts with "Hi guys" |
| 4-5 | Has a hook but weak — doesn't stop the scroll |
| 6-7 | Decent hook — curiosity or pain-based, but could be sharper |
| 8-9 | Strong hook — pattern interrupt, specific pain call-out, or bold claim |
| 10 | Elite hook — impossible to scroll past, creates urgency to watch |

**What to look for:**
- First 3 seconds (reel) or first line (caption)
- Does it call out a specific pain?
- Does it create curiosity gap?
- Does it pattern-interrupt?

### 2. CTA Clarity (1-10)
| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | No CTA / vague ("follow for more") |
| 4-5 | Has CTA but unclear action — viewer doesn't know what to do |
| 6-7 | Clear CTA but not compelling — "link in bio" without reason |
| 8-9 | Strong CTA — specific keyword, clear benefit, urgency |
| 10 | CTA that drives action — keyword CTA with value prop, tested and converting |

**What to look for:**
- Is there a clear next step?
- Does it use a keyword CTA (comment X to get Y)?
- Is the CTA aligned with their offer?
- Does the CTA lead to a free resource / value piece?

### 3. Niche Alignment (1-10)
| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | Off-niche — generic fitness tips, no ICP targeting |
| 4-5 | Partially aligned — fitness content but not for their specific ICP |
| 6-7 | Aligned — speaks to their ICP but doesn't differentiate |
| 8-9 | Strong alignment — specific to ICP pain/dream, references their unique mechanism |
| 10 | Perfect — only THEIR ICP would resonate, everyone else scrolls past |

**What to look for:**
- Would their ideal client feel "this is for ME"?
- Does it reference specific ICP problems?
- Does it position their unique method/approach?
- Is the language matching their ICP's vocabulary?

### 4. Engagement Rate (1-10)
| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | < 1% engagement (likes + comments + saves + shares / followers) |
| 4-5 | 1-2% engagement |
| 6-7 | 2-4% engagement |
| 8-9 | 4-7% engagement |
| 10 | 7%+ engagement or significant saves/shares ratio |

**Engagement formula:**
```
Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments + Saves + Shares) / Followers × 100
```

**Bonus indicators:**
- High save rate = valuable content
- High share rate = relatable/funny content
- Comment quality matters more than quantity (DMs asking questions > "🔥")

### 5. Content Pillar Fit (1-10)
| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | No identifiable pillar — random topic |
| 4-5 | Loosely fits a pillar but execution is off |
| 6-7 | Fits a pillar clearly |
| 8-9 | Strong pillar execution — builds authority or drives leads |
| 10 | Perfect pillar — combines authority + lead gen + brand voice |

**Standard content pillars for coaches:**
1. **Authority** — proof, results, expertise, methodology
2. **Connection** — personal story, vulnerability, behind-the-scenes
3. **Education** — tips, how-tos, myth-busting, frameworks
4. **Social Proof** — testimonials, transformations, client wins
5. **CTA/Conversion** — offers, lead magnets, call bookings
