## How to Use This Data

1. **Content Calendar** → Map top pain points to weekly content slots
2. **Belief Chain** → Each pain point = potential TOF content that calls out their reality
3. **Sales Script** → Use verbatim language in discovery questions
4. **Setter DMs** → Reference specific pain points to show you understand
5. **VSL Script** → Pain section uses exact phrases from this research
6. **Ad Copy** → Headlines that call out top 3 pain points
7. **YouTube** → Create long-form content around most-referenced topics
