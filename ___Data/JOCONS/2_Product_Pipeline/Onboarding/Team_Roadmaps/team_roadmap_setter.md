# Team Roadmap — Setter (DM Sales)

> SOP harian untuk Setter yang manage Instagram DM outreach, lead qualification, dan booking calls.

---

## Role Summary

Setter adalah frontline sales. Tugasnya: find ICPs, build genuine relationships via DM, qualify leads, dan book discovery calls. NEVER be salesy — always be conversational.

---

## 14-Day Sprint (Per Client Baru)

### Day 10-12 — Setter Onboarding

**Terima dari Jordan:**
- [ ] 6 DM script documents:
  1. `cold_scraping.md` — Cold ICP outreach scripts
  2. `warm_scraping.md` — Engaged follower scripts
  3. `sequence_notes.md` — Follow-up cadence rules
  4. `dm_structure.md` — Full conversation flow
  5. `disqualification.md` — Graceful exit scripts
  6. `icp_criteria.md` — Qualification checklist

**Setup:**
- [ ] Baca semua 6 documents — pahami flow dari cold → qualified → booked
- [ ] Setup Instagram label system:
  - No Tag = Lead
  - Flag = Follower
  - Lead = ICP
  - Shipped = ICP-LongForm
  - Ordered = ICP-Freebie
  - Paid = ICP-CTA
- [ ] Baca client's swipe file — pahami ICP, offer, dan brand voice
- [ ] Join Setter Call pertama

### Setter Call (Onboarding)
- Sales/Setter Call: kamu di-book bersama sales manager untuk integrasi ke setter team
- Review semua scripts bersama
- Role-play 3 skenario DM (cold, warm, objection)

## DM Flow (Stage by Stage)

### Stage 1: Discovery (Target: 4+ new ICPs/day)

**Where to search:**
- Competitor followers (list provided in swipe file)
- Niche hashtags (list provided)
- Location tags (gym locations)
- Post engagers asking questions on competitor content
- Client's reel viewers who watched but didn't follow

**ICP checklist (harus match 3+):**
- [ ] Posts fitness content tapi inconsistent
- [ ] 1K-10K followers dengan low engagement
- [ ] Ada link di bio ke coaching services
- [ ] Comment / tanya tentang business growth
- [ ] Located in Indonesia

### Stage 2: Engaged (2-3 hari per prospect)
- Like 3-5 postingan terbaru mereka (genuine, bukan spam)
- Tinggalkan 2-3 meaningful comments
- Watch + react to their stories
- Reply ke satu story secara conversational
- **JANGAN DM DULU** — biarkan mereka notice kamu dulu

### Stage 3: Conversation (3-5 hari)
- Buka DM natural — reference specific post / story mereka
- Talk about THEM, bukan tentang kamu
- Minimum 3 genuine back-and-forth exchanges
- Match energy mereka (formal/casual)

### Stage 4: Problem Aware (3-7 hari)
- Guide mereka untuk share business pain point secara natural
- Listen for signals: "gak bisa dapet leads konsisten", "posting tapi gak ada yang DM"
- Validate dan empathize — JANGAN offer solution dulu

### Stage 5: Solution Aware (1-3 hari)
- Mereka discover apa yang kamu tawarkan secara ORGANIK
- Dari profil, reels, atau natural mention

### Stage 6: Interested (1-2 hari)
- Signal: "Gimana programnya?", "Bisa bantu gue gak?"
- Acknowledge specific pain mereka
- Brief overview (casual)
- Social proof
- Soft CTA: "Mau ngobrol sebentar? Gue bisa tunjukin gimana kita ngerjain ini buat situasi lo"

### Stage 7-9: Booked → Showed → Closed
- Kirim Google Calendar link
- n8n auto-trigger: WA reminder (24h + 1h)
- Follow up no-shows dengan empathy

## Red Flags — Kapan Disqualify

- Tanya harga di message kedua (belum paham value)
- "Nanti aja deh" berulang kali tanpa alasan spesifik
- Serial course buyer (beli banyak course tapi gak action)
- Follower < 500 tanpa coaching experience
- Budget jauh di bawah minimum offer

## Green Flags — Prioritas Tinggi

- Bertanya detail tentang pricing / process
- Share pain point secara spesifik
- Engaged 3+ kali (likes, comments, story replies)
- Sudah punya klien tapi struggle scaling
- Mention competitor atau alternative yang mereka coba
