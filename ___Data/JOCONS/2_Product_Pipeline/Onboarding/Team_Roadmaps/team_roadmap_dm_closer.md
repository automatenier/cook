# Team Roadmap — DM Closer (Sales)

> SOP untuk Closer yang handle discovery calls, closing, dan post-booking follow-up.

---

## Role Summary

Closer menerima qualified leads dari Setter, menjalankan discovery call via Zoom/Google Meet, dan closing deals. Closer fokus pada: understanding the prospect's pain, presenting the offer, handling objections, dan securing payment.

---

## 14-Day Sprint (Per Client Baru)

### Day 10-12 — Closer Onboarding

**Terima dari Jordan:**
- [ ] Sales call script (Claude-generated, customized per client offer)
- [ ] Offer sheet (pricing, deliverables, guarantees, bonuses)
- [ ] Client swipe file (ICP profile, pain points, objection patterns)
- [ ] DM closing playbook (pre-call DM warm-up, post-call follow-up)

**Setup:**
- [ ] Baca offer sheet — hafal semua deliverables, pricing tiers, guarantees, bonuses
- [ ] Baca swipe file — pahami avatar, pain points, dream outcome
- [ ] Rehearse sales call script (role-play with Jordan)
- [ ] Setup CRM access — pahami pipeline stages

---

## Pre-Call Checklist

Sebelum setiap discovery call:
- [ ] Baca prospect notes dari Setter (pain point, stage history, DM excerpts)
- [ ] Review prospect's Instagram profile (recent posts, followers, bio)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 personalized questions based on their specific situation
- [ ] Confirm Zoom/Google Meet link ready
- [ ] Have offer sheet open (for reference, not screen share)

---

## Discovery Call Framework (17 minutes)

### 1. Rapport (2 min)
- Greet warmly, match their energy
- Reference something specific from DM conversation
- "Gue udah baca sedikit dari chat kita, tapi ceritain langsung dong situasinya sekarang"

### 2. Problem Identification (5 min)
- Ask open-ended questions about their coaching business
- Dig into surface problems → deep problems:
  - Surface: "Berapa klien sekarang? Target berapa?"
  - Deep: "Kalau terus kayak gini 6 bulan ke depan, gimana perasaannya?"
- Let them TALK — your job is to listen and validate
- Mirror their language back to them

### 3. Solution Presentation (5 min)
- Connect THEIR pain → YOUR solution (specific, not generic)
- "Based on apa yang lo ceritain, yang lo butuhkan itu..."
- Walk through the 3-4 most relevant deliverables (not ALL deliverables)
- Show 1-2 social proof examples similar to their situation
- Reference the unique mechanism: why YOUR process, not another course

### 4. Objection Handling (3 min)

| Objection | Response Framework |
|---|---|
| "Mahal" | Reframe: ROI per client. "Kalau dapet 3 klien baru di bulan pertama, itu udah [X]x lipat dari investasi" |
| "Perlu pikir dulu" | Validate + urgency: "Makes sense. Yang bikin lo pikir-pikir apa specifically? ...Slot bulan ini tinggal [X]" |
| "Gak punya waktu" | Flip: "That's exactly why — system ini didesain supaya lo HEMAT waktu. AI handle 90% content, setter handle DMs" |
| "Udah pernah coba yang lain" | Differentiate: "Yang bikin ini beda: kita gak ngajarin doang, kita BUILD sistem lo. Content, funnel, ads, DM scripts — semua jadi milik lo" |
| "Mau tanya partner/spouse" | Bridge: "Good idea. Gimana kalau gue share ringkasan yang bisa lo tunjukin ke mereka? Kita call lagi besok 5 menit aja setelah diskusi" |

### 5. Close (2 min)
- Summarize their pain + your solution in 2 sentences
- Present pricing confidently (no apologizing)
- Clear next step: "Payment link gue kirim sekarang, dan besok kita langsung mulai onboarding"
- If not closing today: book follow-up within 48 hours MAX

---

## Post-Call Flow

### If Closed:
- [ ] Kirim payment link immediately
- [ ] Confirm payment received
- [ ] Update CRM → stage: "Closed"
- [ ] Notify VA to start onboarding process
- [ ] Send congratulations message (WA/DM)

### If Not Closed (Follow-Up):
- [ ] Send summary message via DM/WA within 1 hour:
  - Recap their pain point
  - Recap your solution
  - Include 1 testimonial link
  - Soft CTA: "Kalau udah ready, let me know"
- [ ] Day 2: Share relevant content piece (reel/case study) via DM
- [ ] Day 4: Check in — "Gimana, udah ada kesempatan mikirin?"
- [ ] Day 7: Final push with urgency/scarcity (if genuine)
- [ ] Day 14: If no response → archive, move to cold nurture

### If Disqualified:
- [ ] Update CRM → stage: "Disqualified" + reason
- [ ] Send graceful exit message
- [ ] Keep door open: "Kalau situasinya berubah, reach out kapan aja"

---

## Setter Call Sync

### Before Setter Call:
- [ ] Compile: calls booked, show rate, close rate, objections encountered
- [ ] Note: which leads had the best/worst quality from setter

### During Setter Call:
- Share feedback on lead quality → help setter refine qualification
- Identify patterns: what type of prospect closes easiest?
- Align on handoff process (what info closer needs before call)

| Metric | Target |

| Post-call follow-up sent | Within 1 hour, 100% |
