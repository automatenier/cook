## 6. New Opportunity Breakdown

Position your offer as THE new opportunity (not an "improvement" offer).

**Old Way (what the market is tired of):**
- What's the "old way" your ICP has been trying?
- Why does the old way fail?
- What frustrations come from the old way?

**New Opportunity (what you offer):**
- What's the NEW vehicle/mechanism?
- Why is this fundamentally different (not just "better")?
- What makes this feel exciting rather than exhausting?

**Bridge Statement:**
> "You've been trying [OLD WAY] and it's not working because [REASON]. What actually works is [NEW OPPORTUNITY] because [MECHANISM]. That's exactly what we built."
