# High-Ticket Offer Framework + Positioning Templates

> Source: Main Drive vault. Use as reference when running offer_agent.py or consulting with clients on offer creation.

---

## Quick Checklist ($5k Offer)

1. **Specific Avatar** — "Niche down until it hurts"
2. **Clear Promise** — Power Statement formula
3. **Unique System** — 3-Phase proprietary mechanism
4. **High-Leverage Delivery** — Video course + check-ins, not just hours
5. **Risk Reversal** — Guarantee results

---

## Power Statement Formula

```
Saya membantu [Target Spesifik] mendapatkan [Hasil] dalam [Timeframe] tanpa [Hal yang Mereka Benci]
```

**Bad:** "Saya tawarkan 12 minggu coaching dengan weekly calls dan meal plans"
**Good:** "Saya bantu Busy Professionals turun 20 lbs dalam 90 hari dan bangun Movie Star Body tanpa Cutting Carbs atau Cocktails"

## 8-Step Build Process

### 1. Niche Down
- Target ONE specific person
- Examples: "Overweight Corporate Dads", "Post-Partum Moms", "Actors preparing for camera"
- If you target everyone → you target no one

### 2. Identify Deep Pain Points
- **Daily struggles:** "Clothes don't fit", "Can't stay consistent 2 weeks"
- **Emotional frustrations:** "Embarrassed to take shirt off", "No energy for kids"
- **Perceived obstacles:** "No time", "Travel too much"

### 3. Define Dream Outcome
- **Surface desire:** Lose 20 lbs
- **Deep desire:** Confidence to date again, be a role model, get respected by peers

### 4. Create Power Statement
- Combine Who + Result + Timeframe (see formula above)

### 5. Signature System (3-Phase Mechanism)
- Phase 1 (e.g., Metabolic Reset) → quick wins, first 5-10 lbs
- Phase 2 (e.g., Hypertrophy Rebuild) → muscle, visible shape
- Phase 3 (e.g., Lifestyle Reinforcement) → permanent results
- Naming phases = "category of one"

### 6. Delivery Structure
- Video Training Library (universal topics, no repeat on calls)
- 1-on-1 Calls (bi-weekly, solve specific bottlenecks)
- Direct Support (text/DM for fast questions)
- Accountability (weekly audits of logs/form checks)

### 7. Stack Bonuses
- High-value to client, low-effort for you
- Examples: "Restaurant Survival Guide", "Travel Workout Protocols", "Grocery Cheat Sheets"
- If bonuses alone feel worth the price → main offer = no-brainer

### 8. Remove Risk (Guarantee)
- "Work Until You Win" — continue coaching free until goal is hit
- Aligns your incentives with theirs

## Product-Market Fit Template

### Data Collection Sections

**1. Current Situation**
- Overall problems list (3+)
- Deep-rooted problems (fears, pains — 3-5)

**2. Desired Situation**
- Overall desires list (3+)
- Deep-rooted desires (dream life — 3-5)

**3. USP**
- Why should they know you deeply?
- Why should they trust you deeply?
- Why can you help this specific market?

**4. The Bridge**
- 15-30+ specific steps from current → desired situation

**5. Transformation**
- Summarize: current → bridge → desired in ONE statement

**6. Proof**
- Your life journey (origin → now)
- Client case studies (if available)

**7. Features & Benefits**
For each feature:
- Feature name →
- Benefit →
- Value = $___
