## 3. Conversation Flow Map

> Map every meaningful touchpoint in order. This is the core of the report — be specific about what was said, not just "built rapport."

### Touchpoint Template (copy for each step):

```
### Step [#]: [Short Label]
- **Channel:** DM / Story Reply / Comment / Voice Note / WA / Call
- **Who Initiated:** Setter / Prospect
- **What Happened:** [1-2 sentences — exact move and their response]
- **Technique Used:** Opener / Rapport / Probe / Sauce Drop / Urgency / Pitch / Objection Handle / CTA
- **Their Energy:** Cold / Neutral / Warm / Hot
- **Notes:** [anything notable — tone shift, red flag, breakthrough moment]
```

### Example (from real close):

```
### Step 1: Mutual Connection Opener
- **Channel:** DM
- **Who Initiated:** Setter
- **What Happened:** Opened with mutual connection (Mattheus). Quick talk about how we're connected, smooth transition to biz.
- **Technique Used:** Opener (warm — mutual OB)
- **Their Energy:** Neutral
- **Notes:** Didn't force biz talk, let the connection do the work.

### Step 2: Aired + Follow Up
- **Channel:** DM
- **Who Initiated:** Setter
- **What Happened:** Got aired. Sent 1 follow-up. He didn't reply. 3 weeks later he inbounded on his own.
- **Technique Used:** Patience — 1 FU then let it breathe
- **Their Energy:** Cold → then Hot (inbound)
- **Notes:** The non-pushy follow-up probably kept the door open. If I had spammed, he would've blocked.

### Step 3: Transition to Business
- **Channel:** DM
- **Who Initiated:** Prospect
- **What Happened:** He glazed a bit (small talk), then mentioned wanting to transition into consulting. That was the opening.
- **Technique Used:** Probe — asked about the transition
- **Their Energy:** Warm
- **Notes:** He brought up consulting himself. Didn't need to manufacture pain.

### Step 4: Sauce Drop + Q&A
- **Channel:** DM
- **Who Initiated:** Setter
- **What Happened:** Dropped sauce (free value on his situation), answered his questions about what we do.
- **Technique Used:** Sauce Drop
- **Their Energy:** Hot
- **Notes:** Giving before asking = trust built fast.

### Step 5: Pitch Call
- **Channel:** DM
- **Who Initiated:** Setter
- **What Happened:** Pitched a call to explain the offer, positioned it as basically free. He agreed.
- **Technique Used:** Soft CTA — "let me explain, no commitment"
- **Their Energy:** Hot
- **Notes:** "Basically free" framing removed risk.

### Step 6: Post-Booking Rapport
- **Channel:** DM
- **Who Initiated:** Setter
- **What Happened:** Good post-booking convo about his background, current situation, goals.
- **Technique Used:** Post-booking warm-up
- **Their Energy:** Hot
- **Notes:** This pre-sold him before the call even happened.
```

## 6. Lead Type Pattern

Check which pattern this deal followed:

- [ ] **Classic Inbound** — They came to us ready. Short cycle, minimal objections.
- [ ] **Warm Outbound → Inbound Conversion** — We opened, they went cold, then inbounded later off content.
- [ ] **Slow Burn Outbound** — Long nurture, multiple rapport touches before they opened up.
- [ ] **Story/Content Trigger** — A specific story or reel triggered the inbound. Content did the selling.
- [ ] **Referral/Mutual** — Warm intro through shared connection shortened the cycle.
- [ ] **Price Shopper Converted** — They asked price early, we redirected to value, eventually sold.
